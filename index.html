03 Hackathon

YQuantum 2026

Insurance portfolio selection solved on QuEra's neutral-atom quantum stack. Capgemini × The Hartford challenge.

Reduces a real investment dataset to an 8-qubit portfolio problem, computes the exact classical optimum as ground truth, then trains QAOA on QuEra's Bloqade stack to recover it — with penalty calibration so the soft-constrained problem provably preserves the hard-budget solution. Reached ~10.5% probability mass on the exact optimum at p=3.

  • Python
  • Bloqade
  • PyQrack
  • QAOA
  • QUBO
