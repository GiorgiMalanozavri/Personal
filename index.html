02 2nd place · Best Computational Accuracy

YQuantum 2026

Insurance portfolio selection on QuEra's neutral-atom quantum stack. 2nd place overall and Best Computational Accuracy at the Capgemini × The Hartford challenge, YQuantum 2026 (Yale).

Reduces a real investment dataset to an 8-qubit portfolio problem, computes the exact classical optimum, then trains QAOA on QuEra's Bloqade stack to recover it. Penalty calibration keeps the soft-constrained problem's optimum equal to the hard-budget solution. Reached ~10.5% probability mass on the exact optimum at p=3.

  • Python
  • Bloqade
  • PyQrack
  • QAOA
  • QUBO
